3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

Our apps are designed to work without requiring any personal information from you. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

3.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Local Storage Only: Any preferences, history, favorites, or settings you create within our apps (such as conversion history in Unitr or theme preferences) are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences or local databases. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3.3 Device & Advertising Data

Some of our apps use Google AdMob for advertising. In those apps, the following non-personal data may be collected automatically:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Device Advertising ID (for ad personalization and analytics)
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate location (IP-based, for regional ad targeting)
  • Crash reports and performance data

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Some AGhani Labs apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Via Google Ads Settings

For more information on how Google uses data: Google Privacy Policy | AdMob Privacy

4.2 Google Play Services

All our apps use Google Play Services for distribution, updates, and crash reporting. Google Play Services operates under Google's Privacy Policy.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain each app's core functionality
  • Save your preferences and history locally on your device
  • Improve and optimize app performance
  • Display relevant advertisements (in apps with ads enabled)
  • Diagnose and fix crashes or technical issues

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Local Storage

All user-generated data (history, favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any remote servers that store your personal data. Uninstalling any of our apps will permanently remove all locally stored data.

6.2 Clipboard Access

Some apps allow you to copy results to your device clipboard. This is always a user-initiated action. We do not access, read, or monitor your clipboard at any other time.

6.3 Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

7.1 General Rights

  • Access & Deletion: You can clear your data at any time within each app's settings
  • Correction: You can modify your preferences through each app's settings screen
  • Full Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently

7.2 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, update, delete, restrict, port, and withdraw consent for your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control at all times.

7.3 Opting Out of Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads through your Android device settings or via Google Ads Settings.
